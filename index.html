Invisible

Andrew Shiroma

Computer Science Student

Andrew Shiroma grew up in a house of six, with a younger brother and two cats. He attended Huntington Christian School for elementary and middle school, and went to Brethren Christian High School for three years. At Brethren Andrew was able to send an experiment to space in the International Space Station Program, which exposed him to computer science. With the onset of COVID-19, BCHS closed down due to lack of funds and he finished his high school career at Orange Lutheran High School. He is currently enrolled in Concordia University Irvine as a computer science undergraduate with a minor in mathematics.
